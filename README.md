Luna English - Your AI English Learning Companion
Luna English is a revolutionary Progressive Web App designed specifically for Indian learners who want to speak English confidently. Built with cutting-edge AI technology, Luna provides what traditional English courses can't: unlimited one-on-one conversation practice with an intelligent, patient tutor available 24/7.
Why Luna is Different
Voice-First Learning: Instead of reading textbooks or watching videos, you actually speak with Luna. Using advanced speech recognition, Luna understands your English (even with an Indian accent) and responds naturally, just like a real conversation partner.
Personalized AI Tutor: Powered by GPT-4, Luna adapts to your English level—whether you're a beginner struggling with basic sentences or an intermediate learner polishing your business English. She remembers your common mistakes, tracks vocabulary you've learned, and references your interests to make lessons engaging.
Multi-Language Support: Unique to Luna, you can receive responses in Telugu, German, or Hebrew while learning English—perfect for Indians planning to work abroad or communicate with international colleagues.
17 Specialized Modules: Practice real-world scenarios including Daily Routines, Workplace English, Travel Vocabulary, Healthcare Conversations, and even NCERT curriculum for students (Grades 1-10).
No Judgment, Only Progress: Luna never makes you feel embarrassed about mistakes. She corrects gently, celebrates your wins, and keeps you motivated with encouraging feedback.
